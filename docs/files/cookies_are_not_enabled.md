
To use your Google Account on a browser (like Chrome or Safari), turn on cookies if you haven't already.

**Important:** If you get a message that cookies are turned off, you need to turn them on to use your account.

\
To enable cookies

1.  Click on 3rd party cookie blocking icon at the top right of your address bar 
3.  ![](https://app.imsdesk.com/knowledgebase/images/click_on_3rd_party_cookie_blocking_icon.png)
4.  Click on the **_Site not working? link_**
5.  Then click **Allow Cookies** button
6.  ![](https://app.imsdesk.com/knowledgebase/images/click_allow_cookies_button.png)
7.  You will see browser refreshing. 
8.  Now you can sign in with google
