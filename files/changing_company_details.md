**Update your account details like company name, email address, phone number or address, anytime by following these steps:**

 1. Click **Navigation Menu** at top left 
 2. ![](https://app.imsdesk.com/knowledgebase/images/open_navigation.png)
 3. Click **Organization** menu 
 4. ![](https://app.imsdesk.com/knowledgebase/images/go_to_organization.png)
 5. You can see a form to update your company details. 
 6. If you want to change password, Click the key icon at top right
 7. ![](https://app.imsdesk.com/knowledgebase/images/change_password.png)
 8. *If you created your account using Social login [like Google], then you cannot change your password as these are password less login*
 9. Once you completed editing, click save button 


> You cannot edit your Email address. Please contact Erratum Solutions to change Email
