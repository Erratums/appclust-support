### **Using RFID sensor for employee time tracking**    

*It's very easy to setup RFID to track employees check-in and checkout time*

\
Follow below steps

 1. Buy a USB based RFID reader and its supporting RFID cards
 1. You will need RFID cards as many employees you have, but reader you might need only 1
 1. Connect you RFID reader to you computer USB
 1. Open IMS & go to Modules => Employee
 1. Add new employee and focus on Unique ID field
 1. Now swipe the card through the RFID reader
 1. RFID reader light will blink with a beep sound
 1. Now this RFID card is linked to the new Employee
 1. You can fill other details and handover the RFID card to that employee
