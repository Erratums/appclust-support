but the **Quotations** will be moved automatically into Module **Quotations.** You can access this from Modules as below 

[![](https://app.imsdesk.com/knowledgebase/images/module_quotation_marking.png)](https://app.imsdesk.com/knowledgebase/images/module_quotation_marking.png)
